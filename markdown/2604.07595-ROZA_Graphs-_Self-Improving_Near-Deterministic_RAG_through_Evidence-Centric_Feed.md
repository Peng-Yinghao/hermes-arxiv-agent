## Abstract

We introduce reasoning graphs and retrieval graphs that together form a ROZA graph: a self-improving feedback loop in which accuracy gains scale with gold-passage reuse and efficiency gains scale with candidate-pool overlap. The base model remains frozen. We evaluate on MuSiQue and HotpotQA. Accuracy improves to +10.6pp over Vanilla RAG at 50%+ coverage (47% error reduction). 4-hop accuracy improves by +11.0pp. High-reuse deployment achieves highest accuracy alongside 46% lower cost and latency.
